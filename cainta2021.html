SumBingTik Festival 2021: A Celebration of Cainta's Culinary Heritage

In 2021, Cainta, Rizal, revived its annual SumBingTik Festival after a two-year hiatus due to the pandemic. Named after the town's iconic delicacies — suman, bibingka, and latik — the festival marked a joyful return to tradition and community spirit.

The Grand Float Parade featured 22 creative floats made from recyclable materials, embodying the theme "Pistang Perya sa Cainta." The SumBingTik House Decoration Contest saw Barangay Sto. Niño residents creatively adorning their homes with native ingredients.

Events like the Color Fun Run, Bike Ride, and Youth Can Dance promoted health and creativity, while the Caindakan Street Dance showcased vibrant performances in traditional attire. The festival concluded with a Zumba/Aero Marathon and a grand feast celebrating the feast day of Our Lady of Light.

The 2021 SumBingTik Festival was a testament to Cainta’s resilience, unity, and rich cultural heritage.
